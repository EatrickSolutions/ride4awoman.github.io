Seeds of grace

The seeds of grace program was set up during COVID pandemic to support women to grow their own crops. Twice a year, before the beginning of the two annual rainy seasons, Ride 4 A Woman provides women with the seeds and the required tools and knowledge to plant and grow them.

With the same idea as other programs, R4W is focused on providing the tools and skills to women so they can sustain their families by themselves, rather than giving directly the food to them. "I better teach you how to fish than giving you the fish".

Every woman receiving the seeds and the trainings has the duty to bud another woman of her village so to keep spreading the acquired skills through the community.

Over 120 women have been already in this program since it started. Please, contact us if you would like to find out more or donate to this project.
