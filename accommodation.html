Stay with us

You are most welcome in Bwindi

If you are looking for guesthouse accommodation offering an authentic Ugandan experience within close proximity to Bwindi's gorilla trekking headquarters, then look no further.

Come and stay with us!

RESERVATION ENQUIRIES

You will be warmly welcomed by our team and offered hot towels and fresh passion juice before being shown to your accommodation in our guesthouse.

During your stay you will also have the opportunity to enjoy local food in our restaurant.

When you stay with us you are giving back...

The income generated from our clients is used to support the women and families of Bwindi through the creation of jobs, the opportunity to gain new skills, the provision of safe drinking water for all the community, installing solar panels in houses without electricity and providing access to microcredit for local business development, among other projects.

So, when you stay with us you are giving back to a whole community, helping to drive the local economy and supporting the progress of women and their families in Uganda.

Thank you for making it possible.
