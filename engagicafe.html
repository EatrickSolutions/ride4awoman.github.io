Welcome to Engagi Cafe

Where community, nature, and sustainability meet

The Engagi Cafe is a cafe managed by Ride 4 a Woman, located at the starting point of all gorilla trekking expeditions, in Bwindi Impenetrable Forest.

We offer local tea and coffee, local food (rolex, chapati, samosas, katogo‚Ä¶) for breakfast and lunch, everything at a local price enjoyable by the members of the local community and tourists after gorilla trekking.

The cafe is decorated with Ride 4 a Woman handmade items and offers amazing views of the forest through its beautiful balcony. You can sit, relax and enjoy the songs of birds, see playful monkeys jumping from tree to tree and admire the astonishing trees of the forest.

Come after the gorilla trekking, the bird watching activity or whenever you have free time.

By staying at the Engagi Cafe you are giving back to a whole community, helping to drive the local economy and supporting the progress of women and their families in Uganda.

Your Visit Makes a Difference

Every meal, every cup of coffee, every moment you spend at Engagi Cafe directly supports the women of Ride 4 a Woman and their families. We reinvest all proceeds into community development, education, and women's empowerment programs.

"By choosing Engagi Cafe, you're not just enjoying a meal - you're investing in the future of an entire community and helping women achieve economic independence."
