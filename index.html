Ride 4 a Woman is a Ugandan non-governmental organization dedicated to supporting women facing the challenges of poverty, HIV, domestic violence, single motherhood, widowhood and who never had the opportunity to attend school.

Through a variety of impactful projects, Ride 4 a Woman empowers women socially and economically, and ignites positive change within the community of Bwindi, in south-western Uganda. Every initiative is a step forward to build brighter futures and fostering resilience.

Our projects

to empower women in Bwindi

Employing women

60 women come every day to work and find a place of safety and support.

Microfinance

Microloans to support women in their business and personal projects.

Children sponsorship

Education for children from families that cannot afford school fees.

Safe water

We offer safe drinking water to anyone from the community.

Light a home

Providing and installing solar panels in houses without electricity.

Agriculture

Seeds, tools and training to support women with their crops at home.

Goats program

Emergency funds and economic savings to the families in the form of goats.

Building a home

Constructing and repairing houses for those who cannot afford it by themselves.
