Restaurant

Enjoy Fresh & Local Ugandan Cuisine

Enjoy our fresh and local food

We have a restaurant onsite where you can get local tea and coffee, and service all day.

We offer traditional local food cooked in charcoal kitchen so you can enjoy the real taste of the Ugandan cooking. There is also the possibility to eat continental food if you prefer so, or to adapt the meal to your needs (vegetarian, vegan, gluten free, allergens...).

We have a small bar offering wine, beer and a selection of spirits and, most importantly, a peaceful place to rest and enjoy the sounds of the river and birds of the forest.

Let us know in case you want to come and enjoy in our dining area making a reservation.
