Primary school

Our dream for the community

Horeb Primary School project is the biggest Ride 4 A Woman's dream. It is both a dream and a necessity for our community, as there are still many children without proper access to education. We believe that there is no better investment than education to make a difference in people's lives and to continue empowering women.

Ride 4 A Woman program on Child Sponsorship is currently covering the education of over 270 children in different primary and secondary schools of Bwindi. However, there are still many more children not going to school due to lack of economic resources in their homes. Having all these sponsored children in a school directly managed by Ride 4 A Woman would be ideal, because it would require less economic resources per kid. But it is not only about money and resources. We firmly believe that a new type of school is possible. Besides the standard educational curriculum, which is undoubtedly important, there is the need to engage the students differently, so education goes one step forward.

For us, one step forward education means:

Our vision encompasses five key pillars that will transform traditional education:

Diversity and Inclusion

No kid without school, including children with mental and physical disabilities. Every child deserves equal access to quality education.

Project Management

Educate through project management and hands-on skills. Learning by doing creates lasting knowledge and practical capabilities.

Environmental Conservation

Environment and wildlife conservation and sustainability. Teaching children to be stewards of our planet and its precious ecosystems.

Digitalization

Preparing students for the digital future through technology integration and modern learning tools.

Joy and Love

Educate through love and fun, making school a space of joy where children thrive emotionally and academically.

Aligning with UN Sustainable Development Goals

"By 2030, ensure that all learners acquire the knowledge and skills needed to promote sustainable development, including, among others, through education for sustainable development and sustainable lifestyles, human rights, gender equality, promotion of a culture of peace and non-violence, global citizenship and appreciation of cultural diversity and of culture's contribution to sustainable development."

- SDG 4.7 Target on Quality Education

Our Progress

Ride 4 a Woman already possesses the land in which it is expected to construct the school. We are in the process of getting the drawings, designs and budget from an architecture studio in Kampala. Once this is done, Ride 4 a Woman will start fundraising the needed investments to start the construction.

If you want to know more details about the project, please let us know.
