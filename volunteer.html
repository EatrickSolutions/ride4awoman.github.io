Join us in our work

At Ride 4 a Woman we welcome volunteers. There are a number of areas where we would very much welcome your skills. Read on and email us with your questions.

Volunteer Opportunities

Craft

Do you have craft skills you can teach that will create saleable items?

Health & Wellbeing

Are you a trained professional who can provide a program of education and awareness to increase the health and wellbeing of the community?

Building/Labourer

Can you assist our workers with building, architecture or simply as a creative handyman?

Support a Family

Can you help a family to make repairs to their home? Or to dig their garden plot? Or build some basic furniture? The families would not be in a position to supply materials so please consider fundraising or donating funds towards the materials.

Teaching/Tutoring

Would you like to work with the children to teach or tutor? Consider the Homework Club or create a program that will assist a newly sponsored child to be prepared for school. Or you might like to upskill the women with a program on finances and budgeting.

Agriculture

Are you an experienced farmer? Come and show the women of the community new methodologies and techniques to grow their crops more efficiently and sustainably.

IT Expertise

Do you have knowledge on computing and other IT apps? You can provide your knowledge to improve the digitalization of Ride 4 a Woman or to train the women in IT basics to skill them and offer new ways and future opportunities.

Volunteer program details

Duration

The duration of each volunteering program might be between 2 and 4 weeks. Only exceeding the 4 weeks in special cases for people well known by the organization due to previous collaborations.

Work Schedule

Volunteers will be expected to contribute a fair day's work for 6 days per week (Monday to Saturday).

Accommodation

Accommodation is offered on a reduced rate which includes all meals, tea, coffee and bottled water.

Supervision

Volunteers will be assigned a buddy/supervisor who will oversee the day's activities and accompany her/him.

Requirements

Volunteers are required to be +18 years old, to speak English and to meet the organization's mission and values.
